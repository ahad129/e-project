Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment at a ULTIMA salon?

Appointments can be scheduled by contacting our concierge team at +1 (555) 123-4567 or via email at concierge@ultima.com. Alternatively, you can use the contact form on this page, and our team will respond within 24 hours to arrange your preferred date and time.

What is the process for creating a bespoke fragrance?

The bespoke creation process begins with an in-depth consultation with one of our master perfumers. During this session, we explore your personality, memories, and preferences. We then develop several fragrance sketches for you to experience. The final scent is refined over multiple sessions until it perfectly captures your essence. The entire process typically takes 6-9 months.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer secure, discreet international shipping to most countries. Our white glove delivery service ensures your ULTIMA purchase arrives safely and can be presented in person by our specialist team in select locations. Shipping times and costs vary by destination and will be confirmed during your purchase process.

Can I purchase ULTIMA fragrances online?

Our standard collections are available for online purchase through our exclusive client portal. However, our Masterpiece Collection and bespoke creations are only available through direct consultation with our team. Please contact our concierge service for access to our online boutique.

What is your return and exchange policy?

Given the personalized nature of our products, we do not accept returns on opened fragrance bottles. Unopened items in their original packaging may be returned within 14 days of receipt. Bespoke creations and Masterpiece Collection items are final sale. Our team is committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction and will work with you to find the perfect fragrance.

How can I become a VIP client?

VIP status is extended to clients who have established a relationship with ULTIMA through multiple purchases or bespoke commissions. Benefits include exclusive access to limited editions, priority appointments, complimentary consultations, and invitations to private events. To inquire about VIP status, please contact our concierge team directly.
