Celestial Gold

$850

A radiant blend that captures the essence of a golden sunset. Celestial Gold combines rare saffron from Kashmir, aged oud from Cambodia, and amber resin from the Middle East. Each bottle is handcrafted with 24k gold leaf accents and presented in a velvet-lined crystal case.

Key Notes

  • Top: Saffron, Bergamot, Pink Pepper
  • Heart: Oud, Rose, Jasmine Sambac
  • Base: Amber, Sandalwood, Musk
  • Concentration: Extrait de Parfum (30%)
  • Volume: 100ml / 3.4oz
